Try in appointment

  • Remove abutments.
  • Place the bars inside the 3D printed try in outside the mouth to ensure proper fit and to identify the bar that corresponds to each hole.
  • Attach the bar screws onto the implants and tighten to 15 Ncm with the screwdriver and manual torque wrench (1.2mm).
  • Place the 3D printed try in in the mouth and make sure it fits properly in the bars and ask patient to bite with full force. (If delivering a set of upper and lower repeat steps 2-4 with the lower try in)
  • Evaluate Bite, VD, midline, occlusal plane and overjet/overbite.
  • If the bite/occlusal plane is not ideal, take a new bite.
  • Take the 3D printed try in out of the mouth and fill the housing spaces with light body PVS. (It is necessary to place a thin layer of PVS adhesive on the try in; prior to applying the light body PVS)
  • Seat the try-in completely and ask patient to bite with full force.
  • Gently take out the try-in out of the mouth.
  • Place adjustment requests online and send bars and try in back to DENTCA.

Note: If patient does not have opposing dentition and full force biting is not possible, use manual pressure to seat denture as firmly as possible.
