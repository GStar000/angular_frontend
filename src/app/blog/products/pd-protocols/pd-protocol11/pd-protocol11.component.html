Impressions using stone models

  • Take both maxillary and mandibular impressions with alginate or heavy body PVS.
  • Pour stone models. Trim/relieve models to remove irregular areas.
  • Take a PVS bite and submit your case.
  • Place lip ruler on the incisive papilla & record upper lip measurement, by looking at the numbers from eye level. (If measurement is not possible due to the presence of teeth, place it at estimated height outside the mouth and measure on top of the lip).
  • Find out the desired gum and tooth shade by using a vita shade guide.
  • Log into your account and place order online (If new to DENTCA you will need to register and create an account)
  • Print order Sheet and shipping label and ship everything to DENTCA.
