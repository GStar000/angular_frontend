Final Delivery

  • Remove abutments.
  • Check DENTCA-Temp in the mouth to ensure it seats properly. If not please enlarge holes with a slow speed hand piece and an acrylic bur.
  • Place DENTCA-Temp in the mouth and fill spaces around abutments with auto cure acrylic for temporary Provisionals.
  • Unscrew DENTCA-Temp once cured and grind out excess acrylic. Grind off posterior extensions in appliance
  • If delivering a complete set, Repeat steps with opposing DENTCA-Temp restoration; making sure to check the bite before applying acrylic around the abutments.
  • Position the DENTCA-Temp restoration(s) back in the mouth and screw it in. Adjust the occlusion as necessary.
