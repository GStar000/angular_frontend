Why DENTCA-4

DENTCA-4 digitally revolutionizes the traditional hybrid denture solution. From 3D scanning, to generating a digital model and milling; Dentca-4 utilizes state-of-the-art CAD/CAM denture software and cutting edge technology.

Thanks to its segmented CAD/CAM milled bar technology and monolithic zirconia restorations, the DENTCA-4 System does not require the use of multi-unit abutments, extensive bone grafting or difficult bar fitting/planning; thus making your treatments easy to deliver and reducing the chances of fracture/failure. The segmented and custom-angled bars generated by the DENTCA software, simplify the chairside process yet retain the same strength and functionality than conventional implant supported full mouth restorations.
